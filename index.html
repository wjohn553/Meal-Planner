Weekly Meal Planner

Overview

This weekly plan is inexpensive, healthy, and realistic. It includes a slight caloric surplus for those aiming to gain lean muscle or maintain high energy levels.

Grocery List (~$60–70)

Proteins:

  • Eggs (2 dozen)
  • Chicken thighs/tenderloins (2–3 lbs)
  • Ground turkey or beef (1 lb)
  • Canned beans (black beans, chickpeas)
  • Greek yogurt (32 oz)
  • Canned tuna or salmon

Carbs:

  • Brown rice, whole grain pasta, sweet potatoes
  • Rolled oats, whole grain bread/wraps
  • Bananas, apples, frozen berries

Fats:

  • Natural peanut butter, olive oil
  • Mixed nuts, cheese

Veggies:

  • Frozen veggie medley, spinach, onions
  • Garlic, bell peppers, carrots, broccoli

Extras:

  • Salsa, hot sauce, mustard
  • Spices, chia or flaxseeds (optional)

Meal Plan

Meal Options
Breakfast
  • Overnight oats w/ peanut butter & banana
  • Greek yogurt w/ fruit and oats
  • Scrambled eggs + toast + fruit
Lunch
  • Chicken & rice bowl with veggies
  • Sweet potato + black bean bowl
  • Whole wheat pasta with ground turkey
Dinner
  • Stir-fry with chicken and frozen veggies
  • Ground turkey or bean chili
  • Sheet pan: sweet potato, broccoli, chicken
Snacks
  • Peanut butter & banana
  • Boiled eggs, mixed nuts
  • Greek yogurt, tuna & crackers, cheese stick

Meal Prep Strategy

  • Cook 2–3 cups rice and roast veggies on Sunday
  • Grill or roast 2 lbs chicken
  • Prepare chili or a big pasta dish
  • Store in meal containers and mix & match during the week

Caloric Surplus Tips

  • Add olive oil, peanut butter, or nuts for quick calories
  • Drink whole milk or protein shakes if needed
  • Have 2–3 high-calorie snacks daily
